A crew you can count on.

A&M Masonry Inc was founded in Lawrenceville, Georgia with a straightforward goal: deliver quality masonry and construction work that builders and general contractors can rely on. Over more than a decade in the field, we've built a reputation for clean work, reliable crews, and projects finished the right way.

We operate across Georgia and the surrounding Southeast, taking on residential and commercial scopes that range from brick exteriors and fireplaces to structural framing, siding, and interior finishes. Our crews bring trade-specific experience to every job — from the first measurement to the final detail.

We keep things straightforward: show up on time, communicate clearly, and build it right. That's what keeps clients calling us back — and referring us to their neighbors.
